About Ventura Alloy and Steels Pvt Ltd

Precision. Quality. Legacy.

Ventura Alloys & Steels Private Limited is a name synonymous with quality, precision, and trust in the steel industry. Established in 1965 by visionary entrepreneur Mr. Bharat Dipchand Shah, the company started its journey by stocking and importing Tool and Alloy Steels. With a strategic shift from TMT bars to specialty steels, Ventura quickly established itself as a reliable partner across India.

We are an ISO 9001:2015 certified company and have grown into a multi-crore enterprise under the leadership of Mr. Aashish Bharat Shah. Our commitment to innovation and excellence drives us to serve as Importers, Exporters, Stockists, Distributors, and Suppliers of high-quality steel products.

  • Cold Work Tool Steels
  • Hot Work Tool Steels
  • Spring Steels
  • Die Block Steels
  • Alloy Steels
  • Plastic Mould Steels

We cater to various sectors including automotive, engineering, extrusion, petrochemicals, and forging. Our robust infrastructure includes modern warehousing and packaging facilities to meet bulk industrial needs efficiently.

Ventura is a proud member of renowned industry bodies like IGCC, ICCCI, DISMA, MASSMA, SUFI, TSSIA, TAGMA, CAMIT and more. With Ventura, you don’t just get steel — you gain a trusted partnership built on performance and precision.
